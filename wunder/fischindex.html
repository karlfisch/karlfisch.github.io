About

I am Karl Fisch, a teacher for more than thirty years and a learner my entire life. I believe passionately that sustainability is not just about sustainable energy, but also sustainable lifestyle. I believe strongly that we must - and can - transition to sustainable energy, but I also think we need to build sustainable lifestyles for ourselves and each other. That includes a heavy dose of financial literacy and planning, as well as taking care of others in our community.
